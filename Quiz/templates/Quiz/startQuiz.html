instructions

Nitro-Boost

  1. This lifeline can only be activated if the participant has accumulated a score of at least 5 and has an accuracy of at least 30%.

  2. Enter the score you want to be spent on the lifeline.

  3. The maximum score you can spend is five.

  4. When this lifeline has been activated the marking scheme for the next N questions will be changed. Where N is the score spent.

  5. For example, the participant activated this lifeline on question number six and chooses to spend a score of 3. The marking scheme for the next 3 questions will be as follows: +4/-2, +5/-3, and +6/-4.

Combat Zone

  1. The combat zone will randomly occur two times during the 28 minutes of the test.

  2. When the combat zone is active, each correct answer will slow down the timer and wrong answers will speed up the timer.

  3. There will be three types of timer speeds during this zone, Normal, SlowMotion, and SuperSonic.

  4. The red background around the timer will indicate that the timer has sped up and a green background signifies that the timer has slowed down.

  5. The three types of timers will toggle according to the answers given by the participant. For example, if the timer was sped up during the previous question, but the answer chosen was correct for the current question then the timer will return back to the normal rate.
